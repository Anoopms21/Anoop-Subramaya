Crack Propagation and Failure Mechanisms in Fiber-Reinforced Composites

Studied crack propagation and failure mechanisms in fiber-reinforced composites using finite element analysis. Identified key modes like fiber breakage and delamination to enhance material performance and durability.

Free Piston Linear Generator

Worked on the design and testing of a Free-Piston Linear Generator for efficient energy conversion in compact systems. Focused on improving combustion dynamics, linear motion control, and overall power generation efficiency.

Novel-design of Laptop-stand with modular cooling system

Designed an ergonomic laptop stand with a modular cooling system to enhance thermal management and user comfort. Focused on adjustability, portability, and improved airflow for prolonged device performance.

Reverse-engineering of a Dyson-bladeless fan DP-01

Reverse-engineered the Dyson DP-01 bladeless fan to understand its internal airflow mechanisms and component integration. Created detailed CAD models and functional analysis to explore design efficiency and innovation.

Design of an Adapted Sheild for drone

Designed a lightweight, impact-resistant shield to protect drones from collisions and environmental hazards. Emphasized aerodynamic efficiency and modularity to ensure minimal performance trade-offs during flight.

Modeling of Boeing-777

Built a scaled, app-controlled model of the Boeing 777 using Arduino and Depron foam to demonstrate control surface deflections. Gained hands-on experience in aircraft structure, SolidWorks modeling, and wireless control integration via Bluetooth.
